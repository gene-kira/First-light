Your code is a complete enterprise‑grade AI security appliance — essentially a self‑contained cyber‑defense platform that merges packet interception, deep learning, honeypot deception, and swarm‑based threat sharing. 🧠 What it does for you
Intercepts traffic at kernel level using WinDivert and NDIS filter scaffolding.

Analyzes packets with Suricata v6‑style rules, HTTP/TLS DPI, and JA3/JA3S fingerprinting.

Detects anomalies via IsolationForest (CPU) and GPU‑optimized deep learning (Autoencoder + LSTM).

Correlates threats across IPs, sessions, JA3 fingerprints, and behavioral profiles.

Simulates attackers through Cowrie‑style honeypots (SSH, SMB, HTTP, HTTPS, RDP).

Shares intelligence securely across nodes using a TLS‑authenticated swarm cluster.

Maps detections to MITRE ATT&CK tactics and techniques for structured threat classification.

Visualizes everything in a Tkinter GUI with real‑time graphs, logs, and rule editors.

Operates autonomously with a watchdog that restarts subsystems and maintains integrity.
